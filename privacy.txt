Privacy Policy for Boldify Chrome Extension
Last updated: 12.09.2024

This Privacy Policy describes how the Boldify Chrome Extension ("Boldify", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

Applicability of this Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy applies to the Boldify Chrome Extension available for use in the Google Chrome browser.

Information We Collect and Receive
Boldify does not collect or store any personal information. We do not track or transmit any data regarding your browsing activity, history, or any content you interact with while using the Boldify extension.

How We Use Information
Since we do not collect any information, there is no usage of data to describe.

Data Storage and Transfer
Boldify does not store or transfer any data as it operates entirely offline without any need for internet connectivity, except for downloading the extension from the Chrome Web Store.

Your Choices and Controls
As Boldify does not collect any information, there are no choices or controls to manage.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
